About this publication

An independent fan publication about Nikola Tsolov.

nikolatsolovfan.com is an editorial fan site covering the career of Nikola Tsolov — Bulgarian F2 driver, Campos Racing #6, Red Bull Junior Team. It exists because the best motorsport story Bulgaria has produced in a generation deserves serious, long-form coverage in English and Bulgarian.

What this site covers

  • Race-by-race coverage of the F2 2026 season
  • Career timeline from karting to Formula 2
  • The 2027 F1 seat probability tracker — updated each race weekend
  • Rookie analysis and head-to-head comparisons
  • Long-form features on Bulgarian motorsport history

What this site is not

This is an unofficial fan publication. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or representing:

  • Nikola Tsolov, his family, or his management
  • Campos Racing
  • Red Bull Racing, Racing Bulls, or the Red Bull Junior Team
  • The FIA or FIA Formula 2 Championship
For the official site

Official news, race calendar, partner information, and merchandise are published by Nikola Tsolov's team at nikolatsolov.com. Visit that site for anything official.

How this site works

Race results, standings, and points are sourced from public FIA and Formula 2 Championship publications. Quotes attributed to Nikola Tsolov, team principals, or other figures come from public press conferences, official channels, and established F1/F2 media.

Analysis pieces — the F1 seat probability tracker, the rookie title fight article, the head-to-head graphics — are clearly framed as editorial opinion. The methodology behind the 2027 F1 seat forecast is published openly on the homepage so any reader can challenge the math.

Who runs it

Editor & founder
Nikolay Tomov

Bulgarian motorsport fan, based in Bulgaria. Started this site in 2025 after noticing that no English-language publication was covering Tsolov's career with the depth it deserves. The site is written, edited, and translated by one person.

Languages

Editorial standards

  • Facts vs analysis: race results and points are reported. Predictions, probability scores, and rankings are editorial.
  • Corrections: if you spot an error, email info@nikolatsolovfan.com. Confirmed errors are corrected and the change date noted on the page.
  • Images: credited where possible. Reach out for licensing or removal requests.
